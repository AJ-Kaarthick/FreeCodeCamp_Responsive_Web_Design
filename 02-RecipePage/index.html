Classic Chicken Biryani

An aromatic and flavorful rice dish made with layered basmati rice, marinated chicken, and a blend of rich spices.

Ingredients

  • 300 gm Basmati rice
  • 200 gm Chicken pieces
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons Biryani masala spice mix
  • 2 large onions, sliced and fried
  • 1 cup fresh mint and coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, biryani masala spices, and half of the fried onions for at least one hour.
  2. Wash and soak the rice, then boil it in a large pot of salted water until it is 70% cooked.
  3. In a pot, cook the marinated chicken until it is tender.
  4. Layer the boiled rice over the cooked chicken, top with remaining fried onions, mint, and coriander.
  5. Cover tightly with a lid and cook on low heat for 20 minutes to let the flavors steam together.
